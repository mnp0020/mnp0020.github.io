This junior-level course covered mathematical modeling, analysis, and design of physical dynamic systems involving energy storage and transfer. Example problems and labs included mechanical, electrical, and fluid systems. 
Teaching assistant responsibilities included hosting weekly office hours, supervising lab work, grading lab reports, and preparing weekly review sessions to hone problem-solving skills.
