This course introduced students to foundational principles for the following topics: regression, classification, clustering, neural networks, probability & statistics, and ordinary differential equations. 
The course was led by [Michelle Hickner](https://www.me.washington.edu/facultyfinder/michelle-hickner) with recorded videos by [Steve Brunton](https://www.youtube.com/@Eigensteve/playlists).
Teaching assistant responsibilities included hosting weekly office hours and grading assignments through GradeScope.
