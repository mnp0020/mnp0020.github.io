Research Experience
======
Neuromechanics & Mobility Lab, University of Washington
* Research Assistant, since Sep 2022 
  * Leveraged wearable sensors and machine learning to reconstruct inertial signals of gait
  * Built a data processing pipeline in R to extract gait mechanics and output participant summary reports of motion capture and electromyography measures
  * Applied Bayesian principles and time-series analysis to personalize oxygen consumption and gait asymmetry tests for children with cerebral palsy
  * Created data analysis tools to disseminate in clinical settings for gait assessments
  * Supervisor: Katherine Steele, PhD

James R. Gage Center for Gait and Motion Analysis, Gillette Children's
* Visiting Researcher, Spring 2025
  * Developed a generalizable Bayesian model to estimate oxygen kinetics during walking
  * Collaborated with physical therapists and clinicians during patient visits
  * Supervisors: Alyssa Spomer, PhD and Andrew Ries, PhD

Performance Research Lab, University of Washington
* Research Assistant, Sep 2022 to June 2024
  * Investigated the effect of midsole material in running footwear on center of pressure
  * Supervisor: Cristine Agresta, PT, MPT, PhD
